Meal Plans at Towson University

All-Access Plans (priced per semester):
- Bronze   — $2,875 | unlimited meals | 50 Dining Dollars  | 2 Tiger Meals/wk | 3 Guest Meals
- Silver   — $3,145 | unlimited meals | 100 Dining Dollars | 5 Tiger Meals/wk | 3 Guest Meals
- Gold     — $3,455 | unlimited meals | 300 Dining Dollars | 7 Tiger Meals/wk | 3 Guest Meals
- Platinum — $3,705 | unlimited meals | 500 Dining Dollars | 7 Tiger Meals    | 3 Guest Meals

Block Plans (available at 17 locations):
- 100 Block — $1,275 | 100 meals | 200 Dining Dollars | 3 Guest Meals
- 50 Block  — $750   | 50 meals  | 150 Dining Dollars | 3 Guest Meals
- 25 Block  — $400   | 25 meals  | 100 Dining Dollars | 3 Guest Meals

Faculty & Staff 25 Plan — 25 meals
Add-On Dining Dollars — $100

Eligibility: Residents in mandatory housing and student athletes use the All-Access plans.
Off-campus residents/commuters may choose All-Access or Block plans. Graduate students use
Block plans. Faculty/staff have a dedicated 25 plan.
