Glen Complex: Tower A Dorm Reviews at Towson University
🧐🗯📈
⏰  01/04/21
V
Verified Resident

#52525
4.3/5.0


Overall, residing in Tower A is very beneficial. Before you enter the building if you look to your right there is a nice hangout spot for those who want to meet up or play games there. There is a big building in front of Tower A which is the Ustore consisting of snacks, TU gear, book pickup, toiletries, and so much more so this spot will come in handy. Underneath of the Ustore there is also a bank but that's only if your willing to open an account with PNC Bank. Across from that area, there is a football field that has become all of TU students chill spot. In the opposite direction, right beside Tower A is the Glenn dining hall which is so useful because even if your running late it's fast and easy to grab some food. From Tower A it is very easy to get to many important buildings so our location makes traveling easy and accessible. Glen Tower A is a very secure building because in order to enter you must scan your TU one card and show it at the front desk to ensure your in the right building. The people are very welcoming but using your one card to enter the elevator can get very stressful especially when having lots to carry. The laundry room is very clean, there are efficient payment methods, and it includes vending machines! I believe the common areas definitely need work because they are old fashioned and a little creepy. Your actual room is very spacious but the closets don't have doors. The bathrooms are small the shower is a nice size. I would say Glen Tower A has its faults but it may be one of the most convenient spaces.
